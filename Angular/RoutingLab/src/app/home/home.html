About Our Club

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995, Royal Rider Club has been the premier destination for motorcycle enthusiasts who share a passion for the open road, brotherhood, and the thrill of adventure. Our club brings together riders from all walks of life, united by our love for motorcycles and the freedom they represent.

With over 500 active members across the region, we organize regular rides, charity events, safety courses, and social gatherings. Whether you're a weekend warrior or a daily commuter, seasoned veteran or new rider, you'll find your place in our diverse community.
